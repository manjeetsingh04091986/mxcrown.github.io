Who I am

A scientist, a mathematician, a programmer and an artist. Together, these four traits make me a problem solver and a creator — I get a thrill from crafting algorithms, and I love solving difficult problems. I graduated with degrees in physics and mathematics; two subjects that I am passionate about, and that introduced me to the power of data and statistical analysis.

What I do

Most of the time I am up to my eyebrows in data, exploring and transforming it in preparation for whatever machine learning algorithms I will feed the data to, ensuring optimal results. This nearly always entails a great deal of transformations and engineering, creating visualizations, computing statistics, and writing some new code. The rest of the time I am tweaking and testing said algorithms, improving the system further. The end product is a refined pipeline that ingests data in it's native form, and outputs desired results.
